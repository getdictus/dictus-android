<objective>
Add smooth waveform animation (iOS BrandWaveformDriver port) and configurable sound feedback on recording start/stop/cancel.

Purpose: DICT-05 (waveform animation) and DICT-06 (sound feedback) are the two highest-impact polish features for the dictation experience. The current waveform jumps to raw energy values; the new driver interpolates with smoothingFactor=0.3 (rise) and decayFactor=0.85 (fall) for organic oscillation. Sound feedback uses SoundPool for sub-10ms latency playback of WAV files bundled from the iOS app.

Output: WaveformDriver class in core, DictationSoundPlayer in app, wiring in DictationService and DictusImeService.
</objective>
